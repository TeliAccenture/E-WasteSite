Who are we?

We are a family owned E-Waste recycling company. We're expanding quickly and as well as recycling we sell E-Waste parts that are working. We have a strong focus on sustainability and education so we also do our best to encourage education around E-Waste.

We currently have eight recycling centres where we take all sorts of E-Waste and sustainabily dispose of it.
